    Target:
  • Torque with a peak at 9000 rpm and a flat course between 8500 and 9500 rpm;
  • Power with an ideal course.
Runners all equal for the simplicity of realization of airbox mold, to have interchangeable trumpets (3 types, each of which for a different event: longer for endurance, shorter for acceleration, medium for other events) and to reduce costs.
The lenght of runners is 165 mm and the diameter is 32 mm. To solve the problem of the detachment of fluid vein and losses on the divergent, it was done along 220 mm with an inlet and outlet diameter rispectively 19.5 mm and 47 mm. The half angle is 5°.

Battery Aliant X3.
(Now on sell, the team has been collaborating with Aliant to develop this product starting since the prototype)

    Characteristics:
  • Li-Po;
  • 13.2 V, 12 Ah (Pb equivalent);
  • Weight: 1.15 kg.


Connectors and wires.
    Targets:
  • lightness;
  • low encumbrance;
  • fire and tear resistance;
  • waterproofing.
    Choices:

  • Board Cases:
    • 1544NGY waterproof PVC cases.

  • Connectors:
    • wire→board connectors: (with screw-locking system and high density of pins)
      • DSubminiature 9 pin connectors for can bus and digital signals;
      • DSubminiature 44 pin connectors for analog signals coming from sensors;
      • DSubminiature 24W7 & 8W8 for power signals and supply;

    • wire→wire connectors
      • JWPF automotive connectors with inner-housing lock, solid rubber sealed.

      Wires:
    • Raychem 55A0111 aerospace quality wires, resistant to high amperage and temperatures, in different sizes depending on the application (AWG24 for sensors, AWG18 for power devices, AWG10 for engine starter);
    • CAT 5E STP Flexible for can bus electromagnetically shielded.

  • Cables coverage:
    • heat shrink tubing;
    • cable sleeving.

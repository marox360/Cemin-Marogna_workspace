Steering Wheel & Dashboard



Steering Wheel


  • 3 rotary switches:
    • 6 engine maps;
    • 6 traction control system level;
    • 6 launch control system level.

  • 6 buttons:
    • upshift;
    • downshift;
    • info (to change the info displayed on the dashboard);
    • PTT (push to talk);
    • N (neutral gear);
    • LCS (launch control system enable).

  • 4 paddles:
    • upshift;
    • downshift;
    • 2 clutch paddles;
    • 1 clutch switch.


Complete Dashboard.



Tachometer and Gear Indicator board.


Info Display board.


    Dashboard:
  • gear display (7 segments);
  • 18 leds for RPM;

  • 4 alarm leds:
    • engine temperature;
    • oil temperature;
    • fuel pressure;
    • clutch on.

  • 8 alphanumeric display (to display info):
    • engine temperature;
    • oil temperature;
    • oil pressure;
    • battery voltage;
    • air temperature;
    • front brake pressure;
    • rear brake pressure;
    • balance between front and rear brake pressure;
    • fuel pressure;

    • temporary messages:
      • lap time;
      • map changes.
